Here are my use cases.

1. I want to Meditate with the app using the timer.
1.1 Optionally, Use different types of sounds from a predetermined list for start and end.
1.2 Optionally, Play sounds at different intervals during the meditation.  Validate that the intervals are within the total duration.
1.3 Pause, resume, end the session

2. I want to create custom meditation plays using pre-recorded sessions, with pre-configured meditation types, optional start and end sounds.

3. Pre-configured list of meditation types:
3.1 Vipassana
3.2 Ajapa
3.3 Tratak
3.4 Kriya
3.5 Sahaj

3. Log the meditation

4. Summarize the meditation by type or overall

5. Sankalpa: Goal
5.1 X number of hours of meditation (optionally specific type) in Y days
5.2 P number of sessions (optionally specific type) in Y days

5.3 Either 5.1 or 5.2 at specific times of the day (morning, afternoon, evening, night)
5.4 Manual observance-based sankalpa in Y days, such as brahmacharya or meal before 7 PM, with explicit per-date success or failure marking
5.5 Manual observance-based sankalpa with weekly cadence, such as Gym 5 observed days per week for 4 weeks

6. Playlist: Sequence a few meditations one after the other.
